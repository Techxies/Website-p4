In School Activities

Book Parade

The school book parde is a time where the school dresses up as thier favorite book charecters. The K-2 classes even get to walk around in circles in the top courtyard with their classes to display their costumes in fornt of their parents



Easter Hat Parade

In school easter hat parde the K-2 classes get to walk around in circles in the top courtyard with their classes to display their easter hat, they made at home, in fornt of their parents

Excursions

Taranga Zoo

The Kindergartens get to go on an end of year fun day at Taroga Zoo!

Jungle Buddies

Year 1-2 get to go to Jungle Buddies at the end of the year for a day full of FUN!

Archie Brothers

Years 5-6 have a fun day by going to Archie Brothers, with bowling and an arcade to play in!
